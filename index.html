Jasmin Arensdorf

When I have time for leisure, I enjoy being outdoors hiking in the mountains enjoying nature. Many years ago I had the opportunity to hike the Grand Canyon. This was such an awesome experience that I will never forget. Other outdoor adventures I enjoy include archery, ziplining, camping, snorkling and beach coming with my family.


Another interest of mine is my joy of gardening. A landscape designer assisted me with a landscape design. For the past four years I have planted various evergreen and decidious trees and shrubs following this design. Being out in my garden with a trowel and shovel, planting and getting my fingers dirty is hard work, but there is something about being in nature and hearing the birds chirping. For me it's very therapeutic.


I am very passionate about scrapbooking to capture memories of my family and friends. During cold rainy weekends, I enjoy working on my scrapbooks.
